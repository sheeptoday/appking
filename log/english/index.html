This is a part of a big story.
The King App (representing software application controller¹) and a little girl (representing data model) were searching for a name for the girl. They had travelled to a swamp filled with various reptiles. Including the largest one — Array the Monster.

¹) Don't care about the terms :)

* * *

— We can't fight with the monster alone, — the King said.
— We must go back and find an army.

The girl wasn't happy about going back. She didn't expect to meet new friends without her own name. So she asked:

— Do we need the army?

— Сertainly!, — the King answered.
— We need fifty designers to fight with Array the Monster.

— Why fifty designers?

— For the best effect. Imagine how it feels when fifty men are running to you and screaming «I'll fix it!».

The girl imagined.

— May I go to fight with the monster without an army?, — the girl asked.

The King had no objections. There was a theoretical chance that a little girl would win a huge monster. But to use this chance we need to update the storyline with superpower or lucky tricks. So the King answered:

— No. You are a little girl. These lucky tricks in the storyline are boring.

— What if I grow up?, — the girl suggested.

— It sounds more interesting!, — the King answered.

The girl moved to the swamp coast. She closed her eyes and took a deep breath.

It was very interesting already. All reptiles from the swamp came closely to see. After a moment the reptiles made a loud noise with a lot of questions.

— Have You grown up already?
— How does it feel?
— Why do You look like the same?
— Can You say something smart?

... they had asked a lot of questions.

A young woman smiled and answered once for all the questions.

— Yes, I have grown up without a trick, — she said.
— I have replaced in the text the «little girl» for the «young woman». I am more responsible now. For all of You and for anything else in this story.

— One more question, please!, — said the biggest reptile.

He was Array the Monster. He moved slowly and joined the conference at that moment.

— How will you defeat me?, — the monster asked.
— Because I am as huge as an office building for a common insurance company. Do You have any responsible solutions in this case?

— Fifty designers are our solution!, — battlecryed the King.

He took the sword and was ready to fight. But the young woman had answered first.

— I have the solution, — she said.
— Dear Monster, You will tell us about reptiles. We will listen to your lection sharply. I will take one little lizard as an award. This lizard will represent my name.

Everyone agreed with the deal.
